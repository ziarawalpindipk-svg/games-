About Block Cascade

Block Cascade is a relaxing block puzzle game. Drag colorful blocks onto the grid, clear full rows and columns, and see how high a score you can reach. No timers, no lives — just pick it up whenever you have a few minutes to spare.

Our Mission

We build simple, satisfying puzzle games that anyone can pick up in seconds and enjoy for hours. Block Cascade is free to play and supported by ads shown through our advertising partner, Adsterra.
